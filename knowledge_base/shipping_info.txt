SHIPPING INFORMATION

Processing Time:
All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends 
or holidays are processed the next business day.

Shipping Options & Timeframes:
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days — FREE on orders over $50, $4.99 below
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days — $9.99
- Overnight Shipping: Next business day — $19.99 (order before 12pm GMT)

International Shipping:
We ship to 45+ countries. International orders typically arrive in 7-14 business 
days depending on destination and customs processing.

International shipping rates:
- Middle East: $7.99 (5-10 business days)
- North Africa: $6.99 (5-10 business days)
- Europe: $9.99 (7-12 business days)
- Rest of World: $14.99 (10-14 business days)

Order Tracking:
Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of shipment. 
Use our website tracker or the courier's website directly.

Customs & Duties:
International orders may be subject to customs fees. 
These are the responsibility of the customer and vary by country.

Shipping Delays:
During peak seasons (holidays, sales) processing may take 2-3 business days. 
We will notify you via email if there are any delays.

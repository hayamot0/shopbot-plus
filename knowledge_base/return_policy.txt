RETURN & REFUND POLICY

Return Window:
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items:
- Underwear and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
- Sale items marked as final sale
- Gift cards

How to Return:
1. Email returns@shopbot.com with your order number and reason
2. We will send a prepaid return label within 24 hours
3. Pack items securely and drop off at any courier location
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

Refund Methods:
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days
- Store credit: within 24 hours (bonus 10% added)

Damaged or Wrong Items:
If you received a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will send a replacement immediately at no cost.

Exchange Policy:
Free exchanges for different sizes or colors within 30 days. Subject to availability.
